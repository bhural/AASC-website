Objectives

Our Objectives and Mission and

The Arab African Supreme Council’s mission is to promote unity and mutual understanding between Arab and African countries. Its core objectives include strengthening diplomatic relations, boosting economic cooperation, enhancing cultural exchange, and addressing common regional challenges.

Diplomatic Relations

Facilitate diplomatic dialogues and negotiations between Arab and African nations to strengthen international relations..

Economic Cooperation

Implement programs and initiatives to enhance economic collaboration, trade, and investment opportunities between member states.

Cultural Exchange Programs

Organize and support cultural exchange events, art exhibitions, festivals, and educational programs.

Regional Challenges

Address shared political, social, and economic challenges by fostering collaboration,and initiatives to find solutions.

Education & Research

Support educational initiatives, scholarships, and collaborative research projects to enhance knowledge

Infrastructure Development

joint infrastructure projects to improve connectivity, transportation, and development in regions.

Healthcare Cooperation

Collaborate on healthcare initiatives, share medical expertise, and work together.

Technology Transfer

Promote the exchange of technology, innovation, and expertise to accelerate technological advancements in the region.

Environmental Sustainability

Collaborate on environmental conservation efforts, address climate change issues, and work towards sustainable development practices.
